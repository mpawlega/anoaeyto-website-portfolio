Brand Development & Digital Design

Tea and Culture Club

I built the complete visual identity for the University of Waterloo Tea Club over one year. Working across print, social media, and video, I grew the community by 600+ followers—designing event posters, stickers, and weekly content while collaborating with other campus clubs to create a distinctive, recognizable brand presence.

01

What I Did

◈ SOCIAL MEDIA

Weekly Content & Growth

Managed weekly Instagram posts featuring club meeting information and custom visual designs. Grew follower count by 600+ over one year while evolving visual identity. Designed posts in Photoshop using Blender 3D assets and custom colour gradients for cohesive brand presence.

◈ COLLABORATIONS

Cross-Club Events

Partnered with Cheese Club and DJ Club to create cohesive visual branding for joint events in premium campus locations. Curated and released collaborative playlists to extend brand experience beyond visual design.

◈ VIDEO PRODUCTION

Reels & Event Capture

Produced and edited short-form video content for Instagram, capturing event highlights and club culture. Shot videos in-house and edited using Adobe Premiere Pro to maintain brand consistency across all platforms.

◈ PRINT COLLATERAL

Posters & Stickers

Designed and produced 100–200 copies of event posters and pixel art stickers for club fair and membership distribution. Posters were so well-received that other club booths inquired about the design. Created variants for campus-wide distribution and showcased design iterations in Figma for team alignment.

◈ BRAND EVOLUTION

Visual Identity Development

Evolved brand visual language over one year, experimenting with different design styles and colour palettes. Refined identity based on community feedback and design trends to create a distinctive, recognizable presence.

◈ WEB DESIGN

Website Assets & Visuals

Collaborated with club executive to develop website design assets and custom graphics for the Tea and Culture Club digital presence.
